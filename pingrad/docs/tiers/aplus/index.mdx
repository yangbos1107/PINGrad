# A+ 梯度总览

A+ 档是高冲刺但相对可达的上位档，建议重点投入材料质量。

## 当前分档（合并拟定版）

- [Yale University](/docs/Aplus/yale-msece) — Master of Science in Electrical & Computer Engineering
- [Carnegie Mellon University](/docs/Aplus/cmu-msece) — MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering
- [University of California, Los Angeles](/docs/Aplus/ucla-msece) — Electrical and Computer Engineering (MS)
- [University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign](/docs/Aplus/uiuc-msece) — MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering
- [Georgia Institute of Technology](/docs/Aplus/gatech-msece) — Master of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering
- [University of California, Berkeley](/docs/Aplus/uc-berkeley-eecs-meng) — EECS MEng
- [Cornell University](/docs/Aplus/cornell-meng-ece) — Electrical and Computer Engineering (M.Eng.)

## 建议策略

1. 每所学校准备独立版本的 SOP 核心段落。
2. 强化与你目标实验室/方向的对应关系。
3. 尽量在第一批截止前完成提交。

## 使用方式

- 左侧 Sidebar 可直接展开该梯度并进入具体学校项目页。
- 每个学校页统一包含：项目介绍、录取bar和dp、RA、找工、生活。
