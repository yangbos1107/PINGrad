# A+ 梯度总览

A+ 档是高冲刺但相对可达的上位档，建议重点投入材料质量。

## 当前分档（合并拟定版）

- [Carnegie Mellon University](/docs/a-plus/carnegie-mellon-university) — MS in Electrical and Computer Engineering
- [University of Pennsylvania](/docs/a-plus/university-of-pennsylvania) — Master of Science in Engineering in Electrical Engineering
- [Cornell University](/docs/a-plus/cornell-university) — Electrical and Computer Engineering (M.Eng.)
- [Columbia University](/docs/a-plus/columbia-university) — Master of Science in Electrical Engineering
- [University of California, Berkeley](/docs/a-plus/university-of-california-berkeley) — Electrical Engineering & Computer Sciences MS
- [Yale University](/docs/a-plus/yale-university) — Master of Science in Electrical & Computer Engineering
- [Johns Hopkins University](/docs/a-plus/johns-hopkins-university) — MSE: Electrical and Computer Engineering
- [Duke University](/docs/a-plus/duke-university) — Master of Science in Electrical & Computer Engineering

## 建议策略

1. 每所学校准备独立版本的 SOP 核心段落。
2. 强化与你目标实验室/方向的对应关系。
3. 尽量在第一批截止前完成提交。

## 使用方式

- 左侧 Sidebar 可直接展开该梯度并进入具体学校项目页。
- 每个学校页统一包含：项目介绍、录取bar和dp、RA、找工、生活。
